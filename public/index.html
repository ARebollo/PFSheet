Quick and useful character records on your tablet or laptop

— Alpha Coming Soon —

 

Features

  • Ditigal, enhanced version of the regular paper character sheet
  • Cloud-based storage, access from any device
  • Complete with all OGL spell descriptions right at your fingertip
  • Track prepared and cast spells and spell-likes
  • Share a read-only statblock view with your GM or players
  • Comment on the statblock
  • Annotate feats, gear, and more
  • Records but doesn't assume, house-rules friendly

This character sheet web app is created by Mottokrosh, and is currently in an alpha status.
